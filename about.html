Subrina Zhao () is a multi-talented female model represented in both Los Angeles and Las Vegas. Though now dividing her time between Los Angeles, Los Vegas and New York City, Subrina spent the first sixteen years of her life in China. Born into an artist family, the granddaughter of a famous Chinese artist, Subrina was accepted at a young age to the most prestigious music university in Shanghai, The Shanghai Conservatory of Music.

In 2005 Subrina was chosen by the Chinese Foreign Experts Bureau along with United Students Association to come to America -- Mansfield, Texas to be exact -- as a High School exchange student. Later, she attended FIDM and USC in California and studied at the Institute Catholique in Paris. Subrina is currently finishing her degree at Pace University in downtown Manhattan.

Subrina started drawing when she was three, and remains an avid painter today.

In the words of her teacher Sean Mahoney “(While snowboarding) ...she must’ve fallen down 450 times, but she got back up 451, laughing and good-humored the whole time. She faces forward.”
